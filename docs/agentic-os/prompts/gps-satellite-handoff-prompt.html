GPS Satellite Hand-off — Agentic Operating System Best Practices

Authored 2026-05-11 by the upstream agentic-OS chat. Hand-off target: the chat dedicated to building out the 4 GPS satellites (HV+FVG+GZ1, vob-single-sens, vob-asym at two sensitivity thresholds). This document onboards you with full context, locks in standing decisions, and defines your scope.

Read this whole document before writing a single line of Pine or Python. The framing is everything. If you skip to the deliverables and miss the dialectic correction in SD-010 or the worker-tier contract, you will rebuild the same mistake we just spent two days un-doing.

1. Mission

Anish is running a trading-fund pipeline that will ingest 200–500 TB of historical and real-time market data. The goal is to maximize alpha by externalizing his trading cognition into a multi-agent system. Every agent we build is a node of his thinking; every skill is a recurring mental act he performs when deciding whether to take a position.

The system has two intertwined responsibilities. First, detection: identify when a tradable setup forms, with timing aligned to the true nature of the signal — no over-firing, no under-firing. Second, adjudication: given a confirmed setup, predict whether the market will FOLLOW the setup direction or DEFY it. Both responsibilities must be modular, replayable, and continuously learning.

1.1 Scope guardrails — what we are NOT building

This is a hyper-aggressive growth, alpha-maximization system. Anish 2026-05-11: "We don't deal with anything risk management. We don't deal with the typical hedge fund organizational stuff. That's not our style. We're hyper-aggressive growth. We're maximizing alpha. We don't deal with the bullshit of calculating beta and take profit and blah, blah, blah."

Concrete exclusions:

  • No portfolio risk management. No beta, no Sharpe, no VaR, no Kelly sizing, no stop-loss heuristics, no take-profit ladders. Not because they don't exist — because they're not Anish's edge.
  • No hedge-fund organizational ceremony. No compliance agents, no committee approvals, no audit-trail-as-control-layer. The audit trail comes from append-only logs (SD-002 / SD-004), not from a separate role.
  • No social media, no Chinese platforms, no WeChat/Weibo/Douyin integrations. Some open-source frameworks bundle these — strip them. We are not a social-media product.
  • No "general-purpose AI agent" framing. We are a single-purpose system: read market microstructure, decide trades. Every component justifies its existence relative to that purpose.

When you read DeerFlow / AIOS / CrewAI / LangGraph for patterns, take the orchestration / memory / message-bus / sub-agent-spawning patterns. Discard their default-included risk-management, social-media, customer-support, or generic-research modules.

2. The Brain Analogy Is Not an Analogy

Anish: "If we're not recreating my simplistic brain, what are we really doing here?" The agentic OS literally externalizes his cognition. The bull-case ↔ bear-case dialectic is the atomic unit — not a feature, not a layer, the unit. Build to mirror his thinking, or you are building something else.

The framing for the dialectic is balance, not morality. Krishna preserves; Shiva destroys; both are necessary because creation requires destruction. Bull and bear outcomes are equally valid possibilities at any setup. The dialectic produces a probability distribution, not a verdict.

3. Worker Tiers — The Class Hierarchy

Three classes. Clear contract between them. Grunt workers don't opine; knowledge workers don't tabulate; owners don't do either — they assemble.

Compute operand values via the canonical Python port.Opine, score confidence, debate direction, judge "good" vs "bad."
Tier 1 — KnowledgeStrategic reasoning, dialecticConsume grunt-worker output. Reason over situational context. Produce confidence scores via the bull-case ↔ bear-case dialectic. Project market outcomes.Re-tabulate (trust the grunt output). Make execution decisions (that's Owner territory).

The class hierarchy is enforced by skill composition: detection-plot-diagnosis (grunt) → bull-bear-dialectic (knowledge) → four-square-matrix (output). Each skill does one thing. If a grunt finds itself opining, it has become a knowledge worker; promote it explicitly or split the work.

SD-010 in detail — read this twice

The bull-case ↔ bear-case dialectic runs only after the diagnosis card is finalized. Both sides ACCEPT the diagnosed setup direction as truth. They do NOT debate whether the operands really fired — the canonical Python port computes that deterministically.

They debate whether the market will FOLLOW the setup or DEFY it over the outcome window (next N bars, next session, etc.). A bearish setup that the market defies (price rips up) is the "order block / divine reversal" cell — Anish's "most magical trait you'll ever have in your life."

5. Layered Context — Central Distribution to Peripheral Agents

Context is king. The 4 GPS satellites are the central power of the agentic OS — their triangulated read of "what level are we at right now" is the context every knowledge worker in the system consumes before reasoning. Grunt workers do NOT consume this context (they just tabulate); knowledge workers and Plot Owners DO.

The distribution topology is hub-and-spoke:

    GPS Triangulator (hub)
   /          |          \
  /           |           \
 v            v            v
Plot      Indicator     Data-Source
Owners    Owners        Pairs (bull/bear)
(Tier-B)  (Tier-A)      (Tier-1B)
  |           |             |
  v           v             v
[per-fire dialectic consumes triangulated level + cross-axis indicator context]

The hub publishes a level packet per bar: which satellites are firing, in which directions, with what magnitude, and the triangulation verdict (high-conviction zone / mixed / no-fire). Subscribers pull this packet on demand when they need to reason. The packet is also written to an append-only log per bar so the knowledge workers can audit historical context.

Cross-axis context flows bidirectionally. Forward: GPS triangulation → Plot Owner dialectics. Reverse: concurrent indicator fires on a Plot Owner's bar (e.g. PPD + MOAB + Nagasaki + FAUNA + DISP all firing) → data-source knowledge workers so the Benzinga earnings interpreter knows "while this earnings event was being interpreted, every indicator in the suite was lit up bearish." That context conditions the earnings agent's bull-case and bear-case interpretations.

7. Triangulation — What "High Conviction" Means

The triangulator subscribes to all 4 satellites and emits a level verdict per bar. v1 is strict: 4 of 4 satellites must agree on direction at the same bar → high-conviction zone. 3 of 4 or fewer → no high-conviction emission (the dissenting satellite is logged for later analysis).

Why 4 of 4 for v1. Anish: "super strict simple rules now, sophisticate later." The strict rule keeps the v1 triangulation auditable, simple to log, and high-quality. The weighted continuous version comes in v2 once we have enough historical data to score per-satellite reliability priors.

Worked example from Anish: "a bearish B2B Napalm + HV+FVG GZ1 zone-on-zone bearish + PPD + RVOL 1x + PBJ bearish + Zone-F on both VOBs bearish — dude, that's a fucking buy-a-put-call, like, tomorrow." Decode: Sat 1 bear zone, Sat 2 + 3 + 4 Zone-F bear, plus two primary detection plot stacks bear. Triangulation = 4/4 bear; primary plots = 2/2 bear. Conviction maxed.
